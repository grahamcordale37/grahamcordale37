First Fav Anime OP

One Of My Favorite Openings Is Tokyo Ghoul both for S1 and S2 called Unravel and Munou. "Unravel" (stylized in lower cased lettering) is a song by Japanese musician TK, released on July 23, 2014, by Sony Music Associated Records. It was released as his debut single from his second studio album, Fantastic Magic. Munou (無能, Incompetence) is the opening theme for Tokyo Ghoul √A and is performed by österreich.

What's so great about it

The Visuals of both the openings are distinctvely unique to each other and and mangaed to convey the visual symbolism of the series core themes and ideas. The Colors of Blue, Purple, and Black are so well stylized.

Music Critic

The Lyrics in the song go on to be about how the main character is struggling with their own identity as it is presented in the series. There is a large internal struggle in their soul when they are both human and ghoul and don't feel the same person. The Lyrics in the 2nd OP are very much in the same vain.

These Songs convey an immense sadness and dread for the main character that makes the song very appealing.

2nd Fav Opening is Air Gear

One Of My Favorite Openings Is both for S1 and S2 called Chain Lyrics. is a song by Japanese musician Back On, and I know is that they Soundtrack for the game series Jet Set Radio.

Everything Great about it

It highly emulates the teenage youthful rebellion of the series. The Lyrics of the song speak on wanting to be free and spreading your wings to fly.

Music Critic

The Visuals are very simple which shows all the characters hanging out like like regular teenagers and riding around the city with rollerblades which is a good vibe. They are simple for the more grounded realism.

The Music and the Vocals feels very metal to me and good at pumping up.

3rd Fav Opening is High School of the Dead OP

The opening theme for Highschool of the Dead, titled “HIGHSCHOOL OF THE DEAD,” is performed by Kishida Kyoudan & The Akeboshi Rockets. This energetic and intense track sets the tone for the anime series, which combines action, horror, and survival elements.

Everything Great about it

This was one of my favorite songs back in Highschool and got me into Anime Music as a whole and would play it on repeat. The Visuals are very colorful and very much a splatterfest with all the zombies and how it highlights the main cast personalities.

Music Critic

The Music and Lyrics has a cool guitar instrumentation and the vocalist is very loud and bombastic and goes great with the tune in my honest opinion.

It very emulates the popcorn entertainment type of fun that I can always have with this series. It felt very good to rock out to it that I had it as my ringtone.

4th Fav Opening is SAO S2 OP2

The opening theme for Sword Art Online Mother Rosario Arc, It was sung by Tomatsu Haruka, Yuuki Asuna's Japanese voice actress.

Everything Great about it

This was one of my favorite songs back in Highschool too and I really enjoyed the first SAO song but I always comeback to this song since it just hits more and ties more into the story of the arc. Some of the visuals look really solid and drive that point home.

Music Critic

The Music and Lyrics is very in sync with the guitar and the Voice actress is a very good singer and the lycrics have a lot of depth.

It very emulates the more sad tone but happy in the moment as the girl enjoys being with friends in one of her favorite games.

5th Fav Opening is CardCaptor Sakura OP1

Catch You Catch Me is the opening song to the first season of Cardcaptor Sakura, sung by Megumi Hinata under the pseudonym GUMI. The song was originally included in the first album of the soundtrack of the anime, Cardcaptor Sakura Original Soundtrack, which was released on July 23, 1998 and contained a total of twenty tracks, where Catch You, Catch Me was the number one song Album.

Everything Great about it

This became a recent favorite opening because it just sounds very sweet and innocent and was intrested through the studio and creator reputation and enjoyed all the Openings and but feel the first one is the best one.

Music Critic

The Music and Lyrics is very in sync with the Voice actress is a very good singer and the lycrics are just very pleasant and innocent that is very pure

It very emulates a very happy go lucky tone since the main character is a young girl going on adventures to collect cards.
