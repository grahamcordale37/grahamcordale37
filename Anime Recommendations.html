Anime Recommendation of One Piece

The First Anime I like to Recommend is One Piece.

The Basic plot of this series is about a kid named Monkey D Luffy that goes on an adventure to find the one piece so he can be the king of the pirates.

He will have to fight other dangerous pirates and journey to different islands to accomplish his dream

What's so great about it

The Worldbuilding is ever expanding. The Author does a great job building up and establishing a variety of locations. Although he does not show all the locations, the amount he does show are very creative and distinctive from each other such as a island made out of desert

The Overall story revolves around the main character traveling seas to the grand line to get to laugh tale. The Overall Structure starts off geting the crew members and testing the MC to see if they are ready for the seas. They constantly fight a different pirate group with each island they go and the more they travel, the more lore of the world gets dropped and makes it more engaging.

Characters

The Characters are layered and have great interactions with each other and have their own goals The Antagonists have a lot of depth into their plans and how they will execute them and have detailed backstories. The Author does a great job on intermigling the characters and explain how they know each other which adds to the Worldbuilding The Author also does a great job building and establishing the core cast to get attached to.

Powers

The Power system revolves around Devil Fruits which are goofy powers that the author is imaginative with and how they function in context context with how the world works and they are in 3 categories called Paramecia, which canges the body physical properties, Zoan and Mythical Zoan, which lets the user change into any existing creature or one of Myth, and Logia, which changes the body to any natural element.

Halfway through the story, The Author introduces the Haki power system where it functions as the strenght of will power with the indivdual person. There are 3 types of Haki with there own advancements. There is observation Haki where the User can see incoming attacks before they land and than there is Future sight where it lets the user see the future. There is Color of Arms Haki where the user can either coat their hands/weapons in black to enhance attacks and damage logia users or use it as defense by coating their bodies and the advancement is Advance Armament Haki. Then there is conquerors Haki where only few men have where they have the willpower to be a king and knock out otheres who are weak will with a wave of their will power and the advancement is advanced conquerors.

2nd Recommendation of Hunter x Hunter

The story focuses on a young boy named Gon Freecss who discovers that his father, who left him at a young age, is actually a world-renowned Hunter, a licensed professional who specializes in fantastical pursuits and elite member of humanity

Everything Great about it

Just Like One Piece, The Worldbuilding is ever expanding. The Author does a great job building up and establishing a variety of locations. Although he does not show all the locations, the amount he does show are very inspired from New York city and the author calls it Yorknew City

The Story is very basic hero adventure but Togashi does a great job of having a genreal framework on where to take the story and the 1st Arc of the Hunter Exams does an Excellent of Introducing the World, Characters, How the main concept works in different facets, and the overall stakes to expect. The Power system gets introduced in the 2nd Arc and author knows how to subvert expectations and be unpredictable on how he takes shounen tropes.

Powers

The Power system of Nen is very Complex and it varies from character to character but follows a framework with their own specfications. It is very complex system that the characters in the world take time to study and each power is different and there is not a set system but a framework and specification on how the powers work.

There are six Forms or Categories of Nen in total that correspond to the kind of Nen abilities a person is proficient in: Enhancement, Transmutation, Emission, Conjuration, Manipulation, and Specialization. It's like a tactiacal battle since it consists of figuring out the Power, the rules and specifications of their power, and the category the power is in. This makes each fight very intresting and solid character study.

Characters

There are a multitude of characters to get attached and Yoshiro Togashi, the Author, does a lot of work to establish a wide range of Characters to give them their own spotlight and focus, structure the story into arcs that center on the characters or progressing the overall plot.

3rd Recommendation will be Naruto

He is a ninja from the fictional Hidden Leaf Village. As a boy, Naruto is ridiculed and ostracized on account of the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox—a malevolent creature that attacked Konohagakure— that was sealed away in his body. Despite this, he aspires to become his village's leader, the Hokage, in order to receive their approval.

Everything Great about it

Just Like One Piece and HxH, The Worldbuilding is ever expanding. The Author does a great job building up and establishing a variety of locations. Although he does not show all the locations, He is able to establish a multitude of information through the names of Villages like Village hidden in Leaf, Sand, Sound, Mist, etc that are based in natural elements that off a variety of cultures and subcultures.

The Story is very focused on the title character bettering himself in Ninjustsu and dveloping his skill. It also involves being to a lot of the World's different evils like War and secret organizations. A lot of the story is center on the main character ninja squad getting used to being Ninja and it esclates into bigger and bigger threats that they have to deal with.

Powers

The Power system of Ninjustsu is very and Simple Complex and it varies from character to character but follows a framework with their own specfications. The Ninjustsu techniques range from commonly known techniques taught in their ninja schools like clone jutsus and how to harness chakra, the main power source for techniques, to family style that run in that family tree bloodline like Sharigan and Byakugan. The Main Purposes range from short to long range combat to covert espinage

Characters

There are a multitude of characters to get attached and Masashi Kishimoto, the Author, does a lot of work to establish a wide range of Characters to give them their own spotlight and focus, structure the story into arcs that center on the characters or progressing the overall plot.
